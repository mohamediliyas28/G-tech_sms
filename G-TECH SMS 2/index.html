Transactional SMS Service

A transactional SMS service allows businesses to send important messages related to transactions, such as order confirmations, OTPs (one-time passwords), or payment alerts.

Promotional SMS Service

A promotional SMS service allows businesses to send marketing messages like offers, discounts, and product launches to a large audience. These messages are designed.

Bulk Whatsapp SMS Service

A bulk WhatsApp SMS service enables businesses to send large volumes of messages through WhatsApp to reach customers for marketing, updates, or engagement.

Voice Call Service

A voice call service enables businesses to deliver important messages or reminders through automated voice calls, ensuring direct and personal communication with customers.
